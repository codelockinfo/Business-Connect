About LA Business Connect

LA Business Connect specializes in fulfilling the Small Business Service needs of California Entrepreneurs, Micro-Enterprises, and Established Small Businesses. We are a knowledgeable and experienced business services company with over 14 years' experience supporting entrepreneurs!

Over 1,000 Businesses Serviced Since 2009

LA Business Connect specializes in Entrepreneur & Small Business Services such as:

CA Corporate Filings (Corps C/S/LLC's), Los Angeles County DBA/Fictitious Business Name Filing & Publishing, Business License, Permit Filing, Access to Capital, Loan Packaging, and Business consulting.
